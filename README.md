# INDUSNET-B2B-G2B-Talent-Sourcing-Platform
Overview
INDUSNET is a web-based platform designed to bridge the critical labor shortage in the Israeli industry by connecting industry HR professionals and recruiters with non-profit, governmental, and public sector organizations. The platform serves as a B2B and G2B gateway, providing a free, centralized channel to tap into new, diverse talent pools.

My Role & Responsibilities
Product & Project Management: 
Initiated the concept based on market needs and personal industry network. Managed the end-to-end development cycle directly with an external software development company and its CEO.

Requirements & Design: 
Authored comprehensive Business and Product Requirements Documents (BRD/PRD) and collaborated with a graphic designer to define and establish the platform's UX/UI.

Strategy & Operations: 
Defined strict screening criteria and workflows for onboarding partner organizations.

Go-to-Market: 
Led the platform's marketing and launch strategy across various media channels, including producing and hosting a dedicated webinar to drive user adoption among industry leaders.

Key Features

Targeted categorization of public/social organizations by specific demographic profiles.
Accessible database featuring organizational data, collaboration opportunities, and direct POC contact details.

Structured onboarding and filtering process for partner organizations.
